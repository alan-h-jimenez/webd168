About the reviewer

Food has always been more than sustenance to me; it's a cultural bridge, connecting people and places. My love for cooking and eating has taken me on countless culinary adventures, where I've discovered the power of a well-crafted meal. As a passionate food enthusiast, I'm committed to sharing my experiences and insights, offering a personal perspective on the San Diego dining scene.

  • Traveled extensively in the US
  • Lived abroad in Asia, Latin America, and the Middle East
  • Read food blogs and cookbooks
  • Have taken cooking classes

Restaurant reviews

When reviewing a restaurant, I consider several key factors: the quality and taste of the dishes, the visual appeal of the presentation, the cleanliness and ambiance of the dining area, and the accuracy of the flavors to their cultural origins.
