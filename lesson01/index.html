About Me

I enlisted in the U.S. Army at Chula Vista, CA and completed basic training at Fort Jackson, SC. I had achieved the rank of Sergeant by the time I left the Army after my second enlistment. My Military Occupational Specialty (MOS) was medical equipment repairer.

I was stationed at many locations during my military career including Fort Sill, OK; Camp Casey, South Korea; and Fort Carson, CO among others. I was deployed to Kuwait and Iraq during Operation Iraqi Freedom.

After my military service, I have worked in a variety of positions and occupations. I have worked as a staff accountant, purchasing agent, compliance officer, et cetera. I am currently working as a back-end developer for a small company that primarily caters to government clients.

Mesa College

I have been taking classes at Mesa College for several years now. I completed all the requirements for the Associate of Arts, Mathematics degree and was awarded the diploma in 2020. I found out this year about the Associate of Science, Web Design program, and I enrolled in it for fall 2024.

My current employment has provided me with experience in back-end development. However, it has given me minimal experience in front-end development. My career goal is to become a full-stack developer as I feel that it would give me more career opportunities.
